About Kigali

Kigali is the capital and largest city of Rwanda. It is situated near the geographic center of the nation, and has been the economic, cultural, and transport hub of Rwanda since it became the capital following independence from Belgian rule in 1962.

Known for its cleanliness, safety, and organization, Kigali is divided into three districts: Gasabo, Kicukiro, and Nyarugenge, each with its own unique attractions and characteristics.
