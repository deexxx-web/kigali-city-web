About Kigali

Discover the history, districts, and attractions of Rwanda's beautiful capital

The City of a Thousand Hills

Kigali is the capital and largest city of Rwanda, situated near the geographic center of the nation. It has been the economic, cultural, and transport hub of Rwanda since it became the capital following independence from Belgian rule in 1962.

Known for its cleanliness, safety, and organization, Kigali is often referred to as one of the most beautiful and safest cities in Africa. The city is built on hillsides, offering stunning views and a pleasant climate year-round.

With a population of over 1.2 million people, Kigali is a vibrant mix of traditional and modern influences, with a rapidly developing skyline, cultural attractions, and business opportunities.

Kigali's Districts

Kigali is divided into three main districts, each with its own unique character and attractions.

Gasabo District

Gasabo is the largest district in Kigali City, covering the northern and eastern parts of the city. It is home to many government institutions, residential areas, and natural attractions. The district is known for its beautiful hills, valleys, and modern developments.

With a population of approximately 530,000 residents, Gasabo is a vibrant mix of urban and semi-rural areas, offering diverse living environments and economic opportunities.

District Leadership

Mayor of Gasabo District

Leading the development and administration of Gasabo District since 2023, Mayor Pualine has focused on infrastructure development, education, and sustainable urban planning.

Attractive Places in Gasabo

World Mission High School

kagugu, Gasabo District

World Mission High School is a prestigious educational institution located in the Kimironko sector of Gasabo District. Known for its excellent academic standards and modern facilities, the school provides quality education to students from Rwanda and beyond.

Kigali Golf Club

Nyarutarama, Gasabo District

The Kigali Golf Club is an 18-hole golf course set amidst beautiful rolling hills. It offers a challenging course for golf enthusiasts and stunning views of the city.

Kigali Convention Centre

Kimihurura, Gasabo District

The iconic dome-shaped Kigali Convention Centre is a landmark in the city. It hosts international conferences, exhibitions, and events, and includes a five-star hotel.

Kicukiro District

Kicukiro is one of the three districts that make up Kigali City, located in the southeastern part of the capital. It is known for its rapid development, modern infrastructure, and growing business centers.

With a population of approximately 390,000 residents, Kicukiro is home to several educational institutions, industrial zones, and residential areas. The district has seen significant growth and transformation in recent years.

District Leadership

Mutsinzi Antoin

Mayor of Kicukiro District

Mayor Mutsinzi has been leading Kicukiro District since 2022, focusing on urban development, improving public services, and creating economic opportunities for residents.

Attractive Places in Kicukiro

Sonatubes Commercial Center

Sonatubes, Kicukiro District

A bustling commercial hub with shops, restaurants, and businesses. The area is known for its vibrant atmosphere and is a major transportation junction in Kigali.

Rwanda Institute of Science and Technology

Masaka, Kicukiro District

A leading educational institution focused on science and technology education, contributing to Rwanda's development in these fields.

Gahanga International Cricket Stadium

Gahanga, Kicukiro District

A state-of-the-art cricket stadium that hosts international matches and has become a symbol of Rwanda's growing sports infrastructure.

Nyarugenge District

Nyarugenge is the central district of Kigali City, serving as the main business and commercial hub. It is the smallest of the three districts in terms of area but is densely populated and economically vibrant.

With a population of approximately 340,000 residents, Nyarugenge is home to the central business district, government offices, financial institutions, and many historical sites. It represents the historical heart of Kigali.

District Leadership

Kayisime Nzaramba

Mayor of Nyarugenge District

Mayor Kayisime has been leading Nyarugenge District since 2021, with a focus on urban renewal, preserving historical sites, and promoting business development in the central district.

Attractive Places in Nyarugenge

Kigali City Tower

Downtown, Nyarugenge District

A prominent commercial complex in downtown Kigali, featuring offices, shops, restaurants, and entertainment facilities. It's one of the tallest buildings in the city.

Kigali Public Library

Central Business District, Nyarugenge

A modern library facility offering a wide range of books, digital resources, and community programs. It serves as an important educational and cultural center in the city.

Nyamirambo sector

Nyamirambo, Nyarugenge District

A vibrant and culturally diverse neighborhood known for its lively atmosphere, restaurants, shops, and nightlife. It's often referred to as the most colorful and multicultural area of Kigali.
